Welcome to Region Östergötland's EHR-prototyping experiments

We are exploring openEHR-based prototyping of EHR (Electronic Health Record) applications.

The focus of our current experiment is on emergency care, where today a mix of paper-based and electronic health record notes are used.

In the initial experiments we are using the EhrScape platform combined with our own code, but hope to be able to test also other platforms.
